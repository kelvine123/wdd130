Beria Kuku Rafting

Our mission: To create safe, thrilling, and environmentally responsible white-water experiences that connect people with rivers and wild places. We train guides, support conservation efforts, and design trips for first-timers through experienced paddlers.

History

Founded in 2010 by a group of river guides and conservationists, Beria began as a small community project to share the joy of river travel. Over the years our company has grown while staying committed to sustainable practices, youth education, and local partnerships.
